My name is Matthew Lawrence and I make games, videos, and other media.

Currently, I am a high school student who has taken up independent game development as an effort of self enrichment. My main project is Enchain, a small, experimental first person shooter which I develop alone. I started out making games at about 13 years old, learning Flash and later Unity + C# over the years.
